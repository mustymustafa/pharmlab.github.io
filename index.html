About

G.E.M

GEM is a Technology company with a platform app set up to mediate between patients and medical facilities by providing key services such as free emergency response, home-based medical checkups and online pharmaceuticals in a bid to change the narrative of healthcare deficit in Nigeria.

We are also passionate about the environment as we strongly believe in climate change, and are looking at ways to create a sustainable green planet through innovation in little changes. We believe earth is a GEM amongst other planets and its environment being under threat brings the need for change which we look to pioneer in the next decade and beyond. We as people are part of the environment and through technology we seek to have a disease free environment by providing home based healthcare services for the world, because everyone is a GEM.
